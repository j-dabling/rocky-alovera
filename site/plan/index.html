Overview

Purpose

To provide an easily hostable, and accesible front-page for all of your other social media sites or projects. Want to write a blog? Put that here! Don't depend on 3rd parties and social media.

Audience

Online creators that want to have more control over their online presence, but want an easy, modular solution.

Wireframes

Create three wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here

Home

The main page can be used to display blog posts, neat current events, or show off recent projects.

Blog

For those that use a blog or journaling feature, there will be an article view, which can be in two modes, annotation mode (featured first) which shows footnotes or other information at the bottom; and concentration mode (below), which focuses entirely on the text of the article.

Contact

Some may just use the site as a front page that links to other social media platforms, or want people to be able to get in touch with them. This can be used to list links (with images and descriptions) to other sites.
